Case Study 1

Wingmate

Project Overview

Online dating has become widely popular in recent years due to platforms like Tinder, Hinge, and Match.com, which make it easy to connect with potential partners. However, there are segment of users who may find it difficult to approach potential dates directly. Additionally, data shows that more than half of young people in America do not have romantic partners, highlighting a need for support in the dating world. Basis the above premises, we aim to develop a dating app that allows users to assign friends who can help and encourage in finding in their dating choices.
